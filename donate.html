Donate for Full Access

Access to our event is by donation to Skullspace Winnipeg. The payment processing portal is PayPal, you don't need a PayPal account, you can click "Donate with a card" to donate with a credit card. After your donation you will be re-directed to the festival access page for donors or also see a link "Return to SkullSpace Winnipeg Inc." with the same. Save or bookmark the donor access page address to skip the donation portal on your next visit. Don't expect a welcome email or "ticket".

Alternative means of donating can be discussed with markjenkinsznc in #SkullSpace on FreeNode.
